What Do We Know?

Despite losing communication with the plane, the investigation was able to reveal more information on what happened to the plane.

The Plane

MH370 was a flight scheduled to fly from Kuala Lumpur International Airport to Beijing Capital International Airport.
The Boeing 777 carried 227 Passengers and 12 crew members.
It was piloted by the experienced Captain Zaharie Ahmad Shah and was co-piloted by Fariq Abdul Hamid.
There were no technical issues detected before the plane took off.


The Disappearance

Less than 2 hours into the flight, Air traffic Control lost all contact with the flight
